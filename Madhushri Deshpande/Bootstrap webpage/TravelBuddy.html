Varanasi (2635 reviews)

Varanasi, situated on the banks of the Ganges, is one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities. It is famous for its ghats, vibrant culture, and spiritual atmosphere. Famous Attractions are Dashashwamedh Ghat, Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and Sarnath.

Jaipur (3542 reviews)

Jaipur is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, magnificent forts, and vibrant bazaars. Jaipur's architecture is adorned with hues of pink, reflecting its historical charm. Famous Attractions are Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, Jantar Mantar, and City Palace.

Mysore (2842 reviews)

Mysore in Karnataka is known for its royal heritage, splendid palaces, and the grandeur of the Dasara festival. It is also a hub for sandalwood and silk. Famous Attractions are Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hills, and Brindavan Gardens.
