Rolls-Royce

Rolls-Royce Motor Cars is a luxury car manufacturer that builds its vehicles by hand in Goodwood, England. The company was established in 2003 and is a subsidiary of BMW Group. Rolls-Royce offers bespoke services that allow customers to create their own custom car

BMW

The acronym BMW stands for Bayerische Motoren Werke GmbH, which roughly translates to the Bavarian Engine Works Company. The name harks back to the company's origin in the German state of Bavaria.

lamborghini

The company is named after Ferruccio Lamborghini, the Italian founder who used his own surname as the name of the company. Lamborghini started his business in 1963 after previously working in agriculture and marine businesses. The company is based in Sant'Agata Bolognese, a small Italian village near Bologna. The Volkswagen Group owns Lamborghini through its subsidiary Audi.
