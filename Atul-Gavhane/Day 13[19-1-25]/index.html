About Bikes

Motorcycles, commonly known as bikes, are two-wheeled motor vehicles powered by an engine. They have been a significant part of human history since their invention, providing transportation, recreation, and an adrenaline rush for enthusiasts worldwide.

FAQs

What is a Bike?

A bike is a two-wheeled motor vehicle used for transportation, recreation, or sport.

How many types of bikes are there?

Bikes come in various types, including cruisers, sport bikes, touring bikes, and dirt bikes, among others.

Why do people love bikes?

Bikes offer freedom, excitement, and an unmatched sense of adventure. Riders enjoy the thrill and connection with the road.
